01 Welcome

You are among the first network participants to experience Scout & Runner ahead of broader release. This Alpha is an early, hands-on phase focused on learning workflows, following defined processes, and providing structured feedback.

Accessing Scout & Runner

When publicly launched, Scout & Runner will appear in the Tasks tab within the Unity App. From there, you will be able to opt in and will be redirected to the Scout & Runner interface via a secure mobile web experience. You will log in using your Unity credentials, for example via NFT or email.

The Scout & Runner interface is accessible on both mobile devices and desktop computers.

Alpha Access: During early and closed phases like this Alpha, access is provided via a direct hyperlink. All activity, including task progress and UP incentives, is automatically linked to your Unity account.
02 Alpha Incentives (UP)

As an Alpha participant, you will be awarded UP (Unity Points) for completing specific tasks during the Alpha phase.

UP-earning activities
  • Submitting research through MapQuest
  • Completing Scout and Runner tasks as instructed
  • Providing accurate and timely submissions
UPs are tracked automatically and granted subject to administrative review and approval, as well as successful completion of any required validation and verification tasks. UPs are awarded as network incentives and do not represent compensation, wages, or guaranteed rewards.
03 What Is the Alpha Testing Version?

This Alpha testing version of Scout & Runner is:

  • An early testing and learning environment
  • A way to understand Scout & Runner roles and tooling
  • A feedback-driven phase that informs future releases
Bugs, interruptions, and incomplete features are expected. This Alpha is not feature-complete and does not guarantee future access, rewards, roles, or continued participation.
04 Confidentiality & Participation Rules

You will have access to non-public content and are bound by the confidentiality terms agreed to in your application. By participating, you agree to:

  • Keep all Alpha content confidential
  • Not share screenshots, videos, or details publicly
  • Follow instructions exactly as provided
  • Not automate, script, or modify any tasks or workflows
All materials in this onboarding package are confidential and intended solely for approved Alpha participants. Failure to follow these rules may result in removal from the Alpha at Unity's discretion.

06 What Is MapQuest

MapQuest is the research and submission module used in Scout & Runner. It is part of the full production build and opens during specific time windows throughout the calendar year.

During these windows, participants submit structured research related to prepaid international call plans and SIM or eSIM offerings.

For this Alpha, MapQuest is included to:
  • Introduce you to the MapQuest workflow
  • Familiarise you with the submission and review process
  • Prepare you for future production MapQuest windows
MapQuest will be the first module you interact with. Detailed guidance is provided directly within the interface.
07 How the Scout Role Works

As a Scout, your role is to perform live validation tasks using approved prepaid international calling plans and SIMs or eSIMs available in the market and procured by the Scout in their region.

As part of this role
  • You will be required to purchase an approved prepaid call plan and/or SIM or eSIM as instructed
  • You will submit proof of purchase through the platform
  • Approved purchases will be reimbursed in UPs following successful submission and administrative review
During the Scout phase, you will be guided through:
  • Registering required information
  • Completing assigned validation tasks
  • Submitting results through reports in the platform
The Scout role focuses on accuracy and adherence to instructions. All task-specific steps are provided directly within the Scout interface.
08 How the Runner Role Works

As a Runner, your role is to verify approved numbers that have already been validated by Scouts.

During this phase:
  • You will be assigned numbers that have passed initial Scout validation
  • You may be required to purchase a prepaid SIM or eSIM as instructed
  • You will perform verification tasks and submit results through the platform
  • You will receive network incentives (UP) for verification tasks performed
As with all modules, detailed instructions are delivered in-platform, and tasks must be completed exactly as directed.

10 What Is Expected From You

Please aim to:

  • Actively participate in assigned tasks
  • Follow instructions exactly as provided
  • Submit honest and clear feedback
  • Report issues, suggestions, and customer support requests
11 Reporting Issues and Support

On every User Interface screen, you will see a floating "Report an Issue" button. Selecting this opens a form directly within the interface — no redirects, no leaving your task.

You can use this form to:
  • Request customer support
  • Report bugs or errors
  • Flag issues or unexpected behaviour
  • Suggest new features or improvements
The Scout & Runner team actively monitors all submissions and reviews support requests and bug reports on an ongoing basis.
12 Communication

Updates and instructions may arrive quickly — please monitor your inbox regularly.

If your availability changes or you are unable to continue, please let us know as early as possible.
